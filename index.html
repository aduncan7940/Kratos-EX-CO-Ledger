EX-CO decision

Read the EX-CR and decide whether an EX-CO is warranted. Required for: BOM changes, part revisions to released assets, assembly changes affecting the net product.

✅ EX-CO is warranted — assign team & categories

A BOM change, part revision, or assembly change is required. You'll assign the ME, EE, PE and Reviewer below.

🚫 EX-CO is not warranted — close EX-CR

Resolved by clarification only. No BOM or rev change needed. EX-CR will be closed with a reason.
